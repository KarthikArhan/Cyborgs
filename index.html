About Project:What is CYBORG?

The term ‘cyborg’ arose as a short form of ‘cybernetic organism,’ which is an entity made up of both biological and technical elements. Initially it was used to describe any system of this mixed type; however, it has more recently been employed specifically for entities where the biology and technology are integrally attached, there by removing people riding bicycles or wearing glasses from the definition.
